Before you ask

The three questions everyone has.

What happens on the 10-minute call? +
Exactly what it sounds like: a short, friendly conversation to see if the cohort is a real fit for where you are right now. You'll tell us what you're working toward, we'll tell you how the program works, and we'll both be honest about whether it makes sense. No pitch marathon, no pressure.
What does the program actually look like? +
A series of live virtual sessions with a small cohort — real conversations, not webinars. You'll learn Onora's Gratitude-Based Leadership approach and put it to work on your team, your project, or your own next chapter, alongside people doing the same.
What if I miss the deadline? +
Once applications close and the cohort fills, that's it for this round — we keep cohorts small on purpose. You'd be waiting for the next one, and there's no guarantee on timing. If you're on the fence, take the 10-minute call: it costs you nothing and holds your place in line.
